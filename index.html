About
SRM IST Ramapuram is one of the top-ranking universities in India with over 20,000 students and 1,500 faculty, offering a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in Engineering, Management, Medicine and Health Sciences, Science, and Humanities.
TecBlaze is a National level technical symposium conducted by department of computer science and applications every year to encourage students to develop technical skills in a fun and interactive way.
